HARVEST FM LESOTHO

Under the guidance and assistance of HCJB World Radio from USA, Harvest fm started broadcasting its signal on May 30th 2003 to residents in Maseru and about 60% of the country, that is, North we cover up to Botha – Bothe and South up to Mafeteng. We have just improved our footprint in order to cover other part of the country. Although we do not have official listenership figures the response to the station has been very tremendous indeed. Our target group is all people of all standards. We still believe that we will enlarge the coverage as we believe God for that.
General LesothoEnglish (U.S.)
